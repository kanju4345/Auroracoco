About us

Aurora Cacao Co

Aurora Cacao Co. is a fictional artisanal chocolate brand known for producing health-focused chocolate products made with natural ingredients, including cacao, plant-based sweeteners, and nuts. The brand emphasizes ethical sourcing, minimal processing, and sustainable production practices.

History

Aurora Cacao Co. was founded in 2018 by Elara Moon and Soren Vale. The concept for the brand emerged in the fictional highland region of Marigold Ridge, where the founders experimented with traditional cacao preparation techniques. The company initially operated on a small scale before expanding its product range.

Founders

"Elara Moon" – Botanist and plant researcher responsible for ingredient selection and nutritional formulation. "Soren Vale" – Culinary innovator overseeing flavor development and chocolate production methods.

Products

Aurora Cacao Co. produces a variety of cacao-based products, including: Dark chocolate bars Nut-infused chocolate bars (almonds, hazelnuts, pistachios) Herbal and spice-infused chocolates Low-sugar cacao blends Plant-based chocolate alternatives All products are marketed as free from artificial preservatives, colors, and refined sugars.

Ingredients and Sourcing

The primary ingredients used by Aurora Cacao Co. include: Single-origin cacao beans Cacao butter Natural sweeteners such as coconut sugar and wild honey Nuts including almonds, hazelnuts, cashews, and pistachios Natural flavorings such as vanilla and botanical extracts Cacao beans and nuts are described as being sourced from small, independent farms using environmentally responsible agricultural practices.

Health and Nutrition Philosophy

Aurora Cacao Co. positions its products as a healthier alternative to conventional chocolate. The brand highlights: High antioxidant levels from minimally processed cacao Nutrient-rich nuts providing healthy fats and plant protein Lower glycemic sweeteners Naturally occurring theobromine for mild stimulation Additional ingredients such as maca root and turmeric are included in select products. Production Process

Production follows small-batch methods:

Roasting and husking of cacao beans Stone grinding into cacao paste Blending with cacao butter, sweeteners, and nuts Flavor infusion using herbs or spices Molding and cooling without synthetic stabilizers

Distinctiveness

The brand differentiates itself through: Use of whole nuts and minimal additives Artisan manufacturing techniques Emphasis on nutritional value alongside flavor Commitment to sustainability

Brand Identity

Aurora Cacao Co. uses eco-friendly packaging with botanical and earth-tone designs, reinforcing its focus on wellness and environmental responsibility.
